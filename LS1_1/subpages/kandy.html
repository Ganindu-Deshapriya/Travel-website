Temple of the Tooth Relic

Enshrined in the heart of Sri Lanka's bustling capital, Colombo, the Temple of the Tooth Relic is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a spiritual landmark for Buddhists worldwide. It is believed to house the left canine tooth of the Buddha, a relic of immense significance.

The temple complex boasts intricate architecture, adorned with gold and vibrant murals. Devotees flock to the temple to pay homage and witness daily religious ceremonies. Beyond its religious importance, the Temple of the Tooth Relic offers a glimpse into Sri Lanka's rich cultural heritage.
