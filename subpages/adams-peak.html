Adam's Peak

Soaring above the Sri Lankan landscape, Adam's Peak, also known as Sri Pada, is a revered pilgrimage site for over 2,000 years. Climbers embark on a challenging nighttime ascent to reach the summit by sunrise, witnessing a breathtaking spectacle of colors painting the sky.

The mountain holds deep significance across religions. A sacred footprint at the peak is attributed to Buddha by Buddhists, Shiva by Hindus, and Adam by Muslims and Christians. Beyond religious beliefs, the trek offers stunning views and a profound sense of accomplishment.
